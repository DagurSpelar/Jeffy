📖 The Story

Summer School Just Got Way Worse

Jackie Chu is heading off to see the Great Wall of China, and needs someone to run his summer school while he's gone. For some reason… he picks Jeffy. (Probably for the views.)

Meanwhile, Mario is looking everywhere for Jeffy — and finds him locked inside the school. Now Mario is trapped too.

To escape, Mario must collect 7 boxes of Cheerios scattered around the school, for absolutely no reason at all. But it won't be easy…

Jeffy is watching. 👀 And summer school just got a whole lot worse. Can you survive and escape before it's too late?
